## About Me

I'm **Amirhesam (Sam) Rayatnia** — a Lead Mobile Engineer and Software Architect based in **Munich, Germany** with over a decade building products that reach people at scale. My work spans iOS platform engineering, systems performance, AR/VR, and FinTech, always with a focus on clean architecture and impact.

Outside of engineering, I have a deep interest in **computational neuroscience** and complex systems — I hold an M.Sc. in Computer Systems Architecture, wrote my thesis on brain network modelling using complex fractal networks, and served as Co-CTO at [Neuromatch Academy](https://neuromatch.io), a global computational neuroscience education platform.
